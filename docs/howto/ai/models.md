# Reviewing available models

To see all available {{brand_ai}} LLMs, first navigate to the [{{gui}}](https://{{gui_domain}}) start page, and log into your {{brand}} account.
Then, expand the vertical navigation bar at the left, click on _AI_ and select _On-Demand Models_.

![All available on-demand LLMs](assets/on-demand-models.png)

All LLMs appear on the main pane of the {{gui}}.

You may read more about the characteristics of each [on-demand model](../../reference/ai/llms.md) we offer, and also know about [tokens](../../background/ai/tokens.md) and pricing.
